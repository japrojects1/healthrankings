Everlywell Cholesterol & Lipids Test
Best Overall — Full Panel, Physician-Reviewed, Accurate

Everlywell's at-home lipid panel is the most comprehensive and best-designed home cholesterol testing experience available. A finger-prick blood sample is collected with a clearly designed kit, mailed to a CLIA-certified laboratory, and results are reviewed by a licensed physician before delivery through their app. The results panel includes total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, triglycerides, and total/HDL ratio — all five key lipid markers — with clear risk interpretation (green/yellow/red coding) and specific recommendations. Accuracy within ±10% of clinical laboratory values in independent validation.

LetsGetChecked Cholesterol Test
Best Expandable — Add CRP, HbA1c, Homocysteine

LetsGetChecked offers a core lipid panel that can be expanded into a comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment by adding high-sensitivity CRP (inflammation marker linked to heart disease), HbA1c (3-month blood sugar average), and homocysteine (independent cardiovascular risk factor). For patients who want a complete cardiovascular risk picture rather than lipids alone, LetsGetChecked's expandable platform is uniquely valuable. Nurse support line available for result interpretation and follow-up recommendations.

CardioChek PA Analyzer
Best Instant Results — No Mailing, 90-Second Reading

The CardioChek PA is a handheld analyzer used in physician offices, pharmacies, and cardiac rehabilitation programs that gives results in 90 seconds from a finger-prick — with no mailing, no waiting. It measures total cholesterol, HDL, and triglycerides directly, and calculates LDL using the Friedewald equation. Accuracy within ±15% of laboratory values — adequate for trend monitoring. The reusable device has ongoing strip costs but is the best option for patients who want frequent monitoring without repeated lab sends. Used by healthcare professionals in clinical settings.

Walk-In Lab Direct Lipid Panel
Best Accuracy — Clinical Venous Blood Draw, No Prescription

For the most accurate cholesterol reading possible without a physician's order, direct-access laboratory services (Walk-In Lab, Health Testing Centers, LabCorp Direct) allow you to order a standard venous blood draw lipid panel online and visit any Quest Diagnostics or LabCorp location — the same facilities and methodology your doctor uses, at discounted self-pay prices. Results are typically available within 1–2 business days through an online portal. This is identical in accuracy to any physician-ordered test because it uses the same laboratory infrastructure.

Pros

  • Standard venous blood draw (most accurate method)

  • Fasting required for accurate LDL
Cons

Fora 6 Connect Multi-Biomarker Monitor
Best Multi-Test Device — Cholesterol + Glucose + Ketones

The Fora 6 Connect is a versatile handheld device that measures six biomarkers from a single platform: total cholesterol, blood glucose, hemoglobin, hematocrit, uric acid, and ketones. While it only measures total cholesterol (not the full lipid panel), it's the ideal device for patients managing multiple conditions simultaneously — diabetics tracking both glucose and cholesterol, athletes monitoring ketones alongside cholesterol, or patients with gout tracking uric acid alongside heart health markers. Bluetooth syncs all readings to the app.
