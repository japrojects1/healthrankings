Omron Platinum BP5450
Best Overall — AFib Detection, TruRead, Neurologist Grade

The Omron Platinum is the neurologist and cardiologist consensus recommendation for stroke prevention patients. Its Advanced Accuracy algorithm detects irregular heartbeats — flagging potential AFib that requires urgent clinical assessment given its 5× stroke risk. TruRead automatically takes 3 consecutive readings and displays the average — the AHA-recommended clinical assessment protocol. Validated by AHA, AAMI, ESH, and BHS — the most comprehensively validated home BP monitor available.

Omron Complete BP7350
Best Advanced — EKG + Blood Pressure for Stroke Patients

The Omron Complete combines blood pressure with a single-lead EKG — the most advanced consumer cardiac monitoring tool. For stroke survivors and those at high AFib-related stroke risk, EKG documentation of an irregular heartbeat is significantly more clinically useful than a simple flag. EKG strips can be shared directly with cardiologists via the Omron Connect app.

Greater Goods Balance Monitor
Best for Post-Stroke Patients — One-Hand Cuff Application

Post-stroke patients frequently have weakness in one arm, making standard BP cuff self-application challenging. The Greater Goods Balance uses a pre-formed cuff that slides over the arm — requiring only one functional hand. AHA validated accuracy, irregular heartbeat detection, and simple large-button interface make it the most accessible monitor for stroke survivors with physical limitations.
