How compression helps neuropathy: Graduated compression (highest at ankle, decreasing up the leg) improves venous return and circulation in neuropathic limbs. The gentle sensory input from compression fabric can reduce the firing of sensitized pain nerves — the same gate control mechanism as TENS. For diabetic neuropathy, compression also reduces edema that worsens symptoms.
