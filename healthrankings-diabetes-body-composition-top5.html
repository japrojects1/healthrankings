Editor's Choice
1
Withings Body Comp
Best Overall — Most Comprehensive Metrics

The Withings Body Comp is the most advanced consumer body composition scale available. It measures weight, BMI, fat mass, muscle mass, visceral fat index, bone mass, water percentage, vascular age, and nerve health (standing EDA). Its segmental analysis breaks down fat and muscle by body segment — arms, legs, and torso — providing clinical-level insight. The Health Mate app is best-in-class with trend charts, goal tracking, and seamless Apple Health/Google Fit integration.

Garmin Index S2
Best for Garmin / Fitness Tracker Users

The Garmin Index S2 seamlessly integrates with the entire Garmin Connect ecosystem — ideal for anyone already wearing a Garmin watch or using Garmin's fitness platform. It measures weight, BMI, body fat %, skeletal muscle mass, bone mass, and body water, and automatically syncs all data to Garmin Connect where it's combined with your activity, sleep, and heart rate data for a complete health picture.

Tanita RD-953
Best for Athletes & Precise Segmental Analysis

The Tanita RD-953 uses 8-electrode bioelectrical impedance — the same technology used in medical and sports facilities — providing the most accurate segmental body composition analysis in a consumer device. It measures fat and muscle mass separately for each limb and the trunk, giving a precise picture of where you're gaining muscle or losing fat. Used by professional athletes and sports medicine clinics worldwide.
