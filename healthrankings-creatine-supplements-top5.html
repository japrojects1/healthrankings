The verdict on creatine forms: Creatine monohydrate has 1,000+ RCTs behind it and is the only form with definitive proof of efficacy. Creatine HCl, buffered creatine (Kre-Alkalyn), creatine ethyl ester, and other forms have not been shown to be superior in head-to-head trials despite being sold at 2–5× the cost. Buy monohydrate — specifically Creapure-sourced or third-party tested.
