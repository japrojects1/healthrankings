Frequently asked questions

Do you accept sponsored product reviews?

No. We buy every product at retail and our rankings are never influenced by brand relationships. Our editorial and commercial teams operate independently.

How can I suggest a product for review?

Use the contact form above and select "Product review request." Include the product name, manufacturer, and where it's available. We prioritize based on reader demand and category relevance.

I found an error in one of your articles. How do I report it?

We take accuracy seriously. Use the form above and select "Correction or factual error." Include the article URL and the specific issue. Our editorial team will review and update within 48 hours.

Are you hiring?

We're always open to hearing from talented health writers, developers, and data analysts. Send your resume and a brief note about what interests you to partnerships@healthrankings.co.
