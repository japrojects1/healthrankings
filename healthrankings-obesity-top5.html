Editor's Choice
1
Withings Body Comp
Best Overall — Most Complete Metabolic Monitoring

The Withings Body Comp is the most medically comprehensive consumer body composition scale available. For obesity and metabolic management, its visceral fat index is the standout feature — graded 1–59 with a clear risk scale, it tracks the exact fat type most associated with metabolic disease. Its segmental analysis shows fat and muscle distribution across arms, legs, and torso separately. Vascular age and electrodermal activity metrics provide additional cardiovascular and nervous system insight. Wi-Fi auto-syncs all data to the Health Mate app without needing your phone nearby.

Tanita RD-953
Best Clinical Accuracy — 8-Electrode Professional BIA

The Tanita RD-953 uses 8-electrode bioelectrical impedance analysis — the same technology used in medical clinics, sports science labs, and hospital settings — providing the most accurate consumer body composition measurement outside a DEXA scan. It analyzes each body segment (right arm, left arm, right leg, left leg, trunk) individually with clinical precision. Preferred by obesity specialists who want reliable data, not just trends.

Best Value — 20 Metrics Including Visceral Fat

The Oxiline Scale X Pro punches well above its price point with 20 body composition metrics including visceral fat index, BMR, metabolic age, protein percentage, and body water percentage. Garmin Index S2
Best for Fitness Trackers — Full Garmin Ecosystem Integration

For anyone already in the Garmin ecosystem — using a Garmin watch, running app, or Connect platform — the Index S2 provides seamless integration that combines body composition data with activity, sleep, and heart rate into a single health picture. 16-user support makes it ideal for fitness-focused households. Weight, BMI, body fat %, skeletal muscle mass, bone mass, and body water all sync automatically.

Renpho Elis 1
Best Budget — Affordable Entry with Strong App

The Renpho Elis 1 offers 13 body composition metrics with a free, well-designed app at the most accessible price in our tested group. It integrates with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and Samsung Health — making it easy to add body composition data to any existing health tracking setup. While BIA accuracy isn't clinical grade, the trend data over time is valuable and the zero barrier to entry makes daily weighing effortless.
