Why BP monitors are essential for AFib: Hypertension is the #1 modifiable AFib risk factor. Daily monitoring tracks treatment effectiveness and detects BP spikes that trigger episodes. Modern monitors with validated irregular heartbeat detection serve as between-visit AFib screening tools — flagging potential episodes for clinical follow-up.

Validated by AHA/AAMI/ESH/BHS — the most comprehensively validated home BP monitor available.
