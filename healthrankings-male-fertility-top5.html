What home tests can and can't measure: The best home tests measure sperm concentration and motility — the two most important fertility parameters. Morphology requires laboratory microscopy and cannot currently be assessed at home. No home test replaces a full clinical semen analysis, but they provide valuable first-step information.

Legacy Male Fertility Test (At-Home + Lab)
Best Overall — Clinical-Grade Semen Analysis Mailed to Lab

Legacy's at-home collection kit is mailed to their CLIA-certified andrology laboratory where it undergoes a complete clinical semen analysis — the same test performed in a fertility clinic. Results include sperm concentration, total count, motility (total and progressive), morphology (Kruger strict criteria), volume, pH, and DNA fragmentation index. A reproductive urologist reviews results and provides a consultation. This is the only home test that provides the full WHO parameter set including morphology and DNA fragmentation — the two parameters no device-based home test can measure.

YO Home Sperm Test
Best Device-Based — FDA Cleared, Motility Video

The YO Home Sperm Test is the most sophisticated device-based at-home sperm test available. It's FDA cleared and uses your smartphone microscope — a clip-on lens with illumination — to create a 10-second motility video you can actually watch and share with your doctor. It measures sperm concentration and motile sperm concentration (MSC), and scores you on a simple scale. The video is the standout feature — seeing your sperm moving is both informative and motivating. Studies show YO achieves >96% agreement with clinical semen analysis for the parameters it measures.

Trak Male Fertility Test
Best Count Accuracy — FDA Cleared Centrifuge System

The Trak system uses a miniaturized centrifuge to spin the sample and measure sperm concentration against a reference column — a more accurate physical measurement than optical detection alone. FDA cleared with clinical validation showing strong correlation with laboratory sperm count. Provides a clear score (Low / Moderate / Optimal / High) with actionable lifestyle recommendations via the app. Best for men who prioritize count accuracy over motility video.
