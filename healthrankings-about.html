Why conditions matter.

A diagnosis shouldn't be the end of the conversation — it should be the beginning. Too many people leave a doctor's office with a condition name and a prescription but no real understanding of what it means for their daily life. That's the gap we fill.

For every condition we cover, we go deep: what causes it, who's at risk, what the symptoms actually feel like, which lifestyle changes have clinical evidence behind them, what medications exist (and their trade-offs), and which health monitoring products can help you track your progress at home.

We believe that understanding your condition is the single most powerful thing you can do for your health. When you know your numbers, recognize your symptoms, and understand your treatment options — you make better decisions, catch problems earlier, and live better.

Our approach to wellness content: Every health article and condition guide we write starts with peer-reviewed medical literature. We focus on practical, actionable information — not fear-based clickbait. We clearly distinguish between established medical consensus and emerging research, and we always recommend working with your healthcare provider for individual medical decisions.

How we rank products.

When it comes to product rankings, we follow a strict methodology designed to eliminate bias. This is how it works:

1

Category research

We consult with medical professionals to understand what matters for each health condition and product category — clinical accuracy, ease of use, data connectivity, accessibility, and more.

2

We buy at retail

Every product is purchased at full price from Amazon, manufacturer sites, or pharmacies. No review units, no manufacturer relationships, no special access. We buy what you buy.

3

Structured testing

Each product is tested against our category-specific rubric. For blood pressure monitors, that means comparing against a clinical-grade reference device. For supplements, it means third-party lab verification of contents.

4

Scoring & ranking

Products are scored across weighted categories (accuracy, usability, value, build quality). The scores produce the final ranking — no manual overrides, no editorial favoritism, no pay-to-play.

5

Medical review & publication

A licensed healthcare professional reviews the final rankings and all health claims before publication. Rankings are then continuously monitored and updated as products change.

Our funding model: HealthRankings earns a small commission when you purchase a product through our links. This is standard affiliate commerce — and critically, it has zero influence on our rankings or content. Products are ranked before affiliate links are added. We would rather lose revenue than compromise a ranking or publish inaccurate health information.

Questions, answered.

What is HealthRankings exactly?

HealthRankings is an independent health and wellness platform that covers three pillars: condition guides (in-depth information about 60+ health conditions — symptoms, causes, treatments, and how to live well with them), product rankings (expert top-5 lists for health devices and supplements, tested independently), and wellness articles (practical advice on understanding and managing your health).

We're based in Miami and operate without advertiser influence over any of our content.

Who writes your health content?

Our condition guides, wellness articles, and lifestyle tips are researched and written by our editorial team using peer-reviewed medical literature. Every piece of health content is then reviewed by a licensed healthcare professional on our medical review board before publication.

We clearly distinguish between established medical consensus and emerging research, and we always recommend working with your healthcare provider for individual decisions.

How does HealthRankings make money?

We earn affiliate commissions when you buy products through links on our site — typically 3–8% of the purchase price. This has zero influence on our rankings or health content. Products are ranked before affiliate links are added.

Our condition guides, wellness articles, and lifestyle tips are entirely free and are not influenced by any commercial relationship.

Do manufacturers send you products to review?

No. Every product we review is purchased at retail — full price, from the same stores you shop at. We never accept review units, early access, or manufacturer-provided products.

How are your condition guides different from WebMD or Mayo Clinic?

Most health sites give you the clinical facts and stop there. We go further — we cover the practical side of living with a condition: which lifestyle changes have the strongest evidence, which home monitoring tools actually help, how to read your own health data, and what questions to ask your doctor. We combine the medical accuracy of clinical resources with the practical, everyday guidance most people are actually looking for.

Is HealthRankings a substitute for my doctor?

No — and we'll never pretend to be. HealthRankings provides health information for educational purposes. Our content is medically reviewed, but it's designed to help you understand your health and have more informed conversations with your doctor — not to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

How often is your content updated?

Continuously. Condition guides are updated whenever treatment guidelines change or significant new research is published. Product rankings are formally reviewed quarterly but updated immediately when products launch, are discontinued, or change meaningfully. Wellness articles are reviewed annually at minimum.
