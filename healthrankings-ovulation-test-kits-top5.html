Best Ovulation Test Kits for Cycle Tracking

We tested 16 ovulation predictor kits (OPKs) — evaluating LH detection sensitivity, whether they track multiple hormones, ease of reading results, app connectivity, and cost per cycle — to find the best tools for identifying your fertility window.

How ovulation tests work: All ovulation tests detect the LH surge — the hormonal signal released 24–36 hours before ovulation. More advanced tests also track estrogen (which rises 2–5 days before LH) to extend the detected fertile window. Digitally connected tests remove the guesswork from interpreting line darkness.

Editor's Choice
1
Clearblue Advanced Digital Ovulation Test
Best Overall — Tracks Estrogen + LH for Longest Fertile Window

The Clearblue Advanced Digital is the most comprehensive over-the-counter ovulation test available. It tracks both estrogen and LH — identifying up to 5 days of high fertility (estrogen rise) plus 2 days of peak fertility (LH surge) for a total 7-day identified fertile window vs the 2-day window of LH-only tests. The digital reader eliminates line interpretation and displays clear smiley faces for High and Peak fertility days. Used in multiple clinical trials and the #1 OPK recommended by reproductive endocrinologists.

Inito Fertility Monitor
Best Data — Tracks 4 Hormones Including Progesterone

The Inito is the only consumer device that tracks four fertility hormones: estrogen, LH, FSH, and progesterone. The progesterone measurement — taken after ovulation — confirms that ovulation actually occurred, not just that an LH surge happened. This is critical for the estimated 8% of cycles with an LH surge but no ovulation (anovulatory cycles). The app provides personalized cycle charts with quantitative hormone values, not just positive/negative results.

Easy@Home Ovulation Test Strips
Best Budget — High Sensitivity LH Strips at Low Cost

The Easy@Home strips are FDA-cleared LH tests with 99% accuracy at a cost of approximately $0.20 per test — the most cost-efficient testing for women who want to test multiple times per day (often the most accurate strategy). The Premom app reads the strip using your phone camera, eliminating line interpretation and tracking results digitally over time. For budget-conscious women with regular cycles who just need LH detection, this is the clear value leader.

Mira Fertility Plus Monitor
Best Quantitative — Numeric Hormone Values

The Mira Fertility Plus is the only consumer monitor that displays actual numeric hormone concentrations (mIU/mL) rather than just positive/negative results — giving you the same quantitative data your doctor uses. It tracks LH and estrogen with an AI algorithm that learns your personal hormone pattern over cycles and predicts future fertile windows with increasing accuracy. Ideal for women with irregular cycles where standard threshold-based tests can be confusing.

Pregmate LH Strips
Best High-Volume Budget — Bulk Testing Value

For women who test twice daily throughout their cycle — the strategy most likely to catch a short LH surge — the Pregmate strips offer 50 tests for under $15. Slightly less sensitive than Easy@Home at the lowest concentrations, but perfectly adequate for detecting the typical LH surge. Pair with the free Premom app for digital reading and cycle tracking.
