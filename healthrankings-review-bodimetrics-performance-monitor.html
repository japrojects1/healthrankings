Expert Assessment

Our 2-Week Testing Verdict

During our 2-week evaluation of the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor, we performed over 100 paired readings to benchmark its accuracy against clinical standards. It earned a 7.4/10 overall score — a solid result that reflects dependable performance for general home monitoring. While it does not match the precision of premium monitors, it delivers adequate readings for tracking blood pressure trends over time.

Bluetooth connectivity is included, allowing the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor to sync readings with a companion smartphone app. In our testing, the connection was generally stable, though we experienced occasional pairing drops that required re-initiating the sync. The app provides basic charting and reading history, which adds value for users who prefer digital tracking over manual logging. However, the app experience does not reach the polish level of premium competitors like Omron Connect or Withings Health Mate.

Priced at $45, the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor sits in the mid-value segment of the market. It is best suited for users who need a functional blood pressure monitor for general wellness tracking rather than clinical-grade precision. The Bluetooth connectivity adds modern convenience that users will appreciate. If you are managing a diagnosed condition, we would suggest considering a higher-rated model, but for periodic home checks, it gets the job done.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor clinically accurate?
The Bodimetrics Performance Monitor scored 7.6/10 for accuracy in our testing. While it generally provided readings within an acceptable range, we did observe occasional deviations of ±5 mmHg from our reference standard. It is suitable for general home monitoring and trend tracking, but users managing a serious cardiovascular condition may want to consider a higher-accuracy model.
Does the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor connect to a smartphone app?
Yes, the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor features Bluetooth connectivity and pairs with a companion app (available for iOS and Android). The app stores your reading history, displays trend charts, and allows you to export or share data with your healthcare provider. Setup typically takes under 2 minutes.
Is the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor worth $45?
At $45, the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor offers reasonable value for its feature set. It scored 7.4/10 overall, which means it performs well for its price tier. More expensive monitors do offer better accuracy, but this model covers the basics well.
How do I get the most accurate reading with the Bodimetrics Performance Monitor?
For the most accurate results: (1) Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measuring. (2) Place your feet flat on the floor with your back supported. (3) Position the cuff on your bare upper arm, 1 inch above the elbow, at heart level. (4) Do not talk or move during the measurement. (5) Take 2–3 readings one minute apart and average the results — this is what cardiologists recommend for the most reliable home blood pressure data.
