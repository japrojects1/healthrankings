1
4th Generation Lab-Send Test (Biophan / myLAB Box)
Best Overall — Earliest Detection, Highest Sensitivity

Fourth-generation antigen/antibody combination tests are the most sensitive HIV tests available outside a clinical setting — detecting HIV-1, HIV-2, and the p24 antigen, which appears 5–10 days before antibodies. This extends detection to as early as 18 days post-exposure vs 23+ days for antibody-only tests. The finger-prick sample is mailed to a CLIA-certified laboratory and results arrive via secure encrypted portal within 1–3 business days. All positive results include automatic connection to a counselor and guidance on next steps.

2
OraQuick In-Home HIV Test
Best for Instant Results — Only True OTC At-Home Test

The OraQuick is the only FDA-approved HIV test that provides results at home in 20 minutes with no lab involvement required. You swab the upper and lower gums, place the swab in the provided vial of solution, and read the result after 20 minutes — one line is negative, two lines is reactive (positive). It detects HIV-1 and HIV-2 antibodies. A 24/7 support line (1-800-HIV-0440) is included for result interpretation and next steps. Sensitivity is 92% in uninfected individuals and 91.7% in HIV-positive individuals — lower than lab tests but significant for immediate, private screening.

INSTI HIV Self Test
Best for Healthcare Facilities & Harm Reduction Programs

The INSTI HIV-1/HIV-2 Antibody Test is a blood-based (finger-prick) test that provides results in 60 seconds — the fastest available HIV test. It achieves 99.8% sensitivity using blood, which is more sensitive than oral swab methods. INSTI is used by healthcare facilities, harm reduction organizations, community health clinics, and NGOs worldwide. While primarily designed for organizational use, it's available for individual purchase and is an excellent option for those who want blood-level accuracy with near-instant results.
