Our methodology

How We Rank Health Products

Every product on HealthRankings goes through a structured evaluation against five weighted criteria. We only include clinically validated devices and products with documented safety records. Our rankings are never influenced by brand partnerships or advertising relationships.

We test products over a minimum of four weeks, validate readings against clinical reference devices where applicable, and consult with healthcare professionals on condition-specific requirements.
