Best Pregnancy Tests for Early & Accurate Detection

We tested 12 pregnancy tests — evaluating hCG detection sensitivity, accuracy at days before missed period, line clarity, and digital readout options — to find the most reliable early detection options.

How pregnancy tests work: All pregnancy tests detect human chorionic gonadotropin (hCG) — the hormone produced by the developing embryo beginning around 10 days after fertilization. Sensitivity is measured in mIU/mL — lower numbers detect pregnancy earlier. Most tests claim 25 mIU/mL sensitivity; the most sensitive detect as low as 6.3 mIU/mL.

1
First Response Early Result (FRER)
Best Overall — Most Sensitive Test Available

The First Response Early Result is the most sensitive pregnancy test available without a prescription — detecting hCG at 6.3 mIU/mL, which means it can detect pregnancy up to 6 days before a missed period. In independent laboratory testing by Consumer Reports and pregnancy community studies, FRER consistently detects pregnancy earlier than any competitor. The curved handle and wide tip are ergonomic and the results window is wide and easy to read. The gold standard for early detection.

Clearblue Digital with Weeks Indicator
Best Digital — Tells You How Many Weeks Pregnant

The Clearblue Digital with Weeks Indicator is the only OTC pregnancy test that estimates how far along you are — displaying 1–2, 2–3, or 3+ weeks (from conception). This is genuinely useful for timing your first prenatal appointment and understanding your due date before ultrasound. The digital display eliminates line interpretation. Slightly less sensitive than FRER (25 mIU/mL) so best used from the day of missed period.

Pregmate Pregnancy Test Strips
Best Budget Early Detection

Pregmate strips are FDA-cleared with 10–25 mIU/mL sensitivity — detecting pregnancy 4–5 days before missed period for most women. At $15 for 50 tests, they're the most practical option for the two-week wait when daily testing feels necessary but using $5 tests is impractical. Pair with the Premom app for digital reading.
