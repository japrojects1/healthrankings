TENS vs EMS for sports injuries: TENS (Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation) — high frequency (80–150 Hz), low intensity, comfortable tingling — blocks pain signals via gate control. EMS (Electrical Muscle Stimulation) — targets motor nerves to cause muscle contractions, preventing atrophy and accelerating recovery. The best sports recovery devices combine both modalities.
