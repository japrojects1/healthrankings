Privacy Policy

How HealthRankings collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

HealthRankings collects minimal personal data. We may collect:

  • Contact information — name and email address when you submit the contact form or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Usage data — anonymized analytics such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, and device type. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.
  • Voluntary submissions — any information you choose to share through forms or email correspondence.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • Send newsletter communications you have opted into (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website content and user experience through aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our platform.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Affiliate Links and Third-Party Commerce

Some product review pages contain affiliate links to retailers such as Amazon. When you click these links and make a purchase, HealthRankings may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These affiliate partnerships never influence our product rankings, scores, or editorial content.

When you click an affiliate link, the retailer's own privacy policy governs how they collect and use your data. We encourage you to review their policies before making a purchase.

4. Cookies and Tracking

HealthRankings is a static website that uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality such as remembering your preferences.
  • Analytics — we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising trackers.

We do not serve targeted advertisements or use tracking pixels.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Our website is served over HTTPS, and any data submitted through forms is transmitted securely. We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from newsletter communications at any time using the link in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at media@healthrankings.co.

7. Children's Privacy

HealthRankings is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at:

  • Email: media@healthrankings.co
  • Or use our contact form.

HealthRankings is based in Miami, Florida.
